About ATHERTECH

We are a university ACM chapter dedicated to fostering innovation and technology for students.

Our Mission

To empower students by providing a platform to collaborate on cutting-edge technology projects, develop coding skills, and foster a strong sense of community in the tech world.

Our Vision

To become a leading student-run technology organization that nurtures future leaders in the field of computing and technology.

Our Goals

  • Host tech workshops and hackathons
  • Collaborate on open-source projects
  • Provide mentorship and career advice
  • Build a strong network of students and professionals

Our Projects

We work on various projects that address real-world challenges and promote learning and development in technology.

  • Hackathon events
  • Open-source contributions
  • Tech talks and webinars
